SSC — Quick Raid Lead Sheet
Serpentshrine Cavern
✅ Hydross the Unstable
  • Tanks swap on marks (4 stacks)
  • Clean transitions = no extra adds
  • Frost/Nature resist tanks required
Wipes come from
  • bad transitions
  • tank not ready → add explosion
✅ The Lurker Below
  • Everyone out of water unless assigned
  • Spout = run, don't panic
  • Submerge phase → kill adds fast
Wipes come from
  • people in water
  • bad spout movement
✅ Leotheras the Blind
  • Demon phase → warlocks tank
  • Inner demon phase → handle own
Wipes come from
  • people failing inner demon
  • bad demon control
✅ Fathom-Lord Karathress
  • Kill order matters (healer → shaman → hunter → boss)
  • Interrupts + control on adds
Wipes come from
  • bad kill order
  • healer add not dying fast
✅ Morogrim Tidewalker
  • Murloc waves → AOE control
  • Earthquake → heal through
Wipes come from
  • murlocs overwhelming raid
  • weak AoE coordination
🔥 Lady Vashj SSC Wall
P1 Tank + spank, control adds
P2 Core tossing (spine mechanics) — Kill striders + naga
P3 Burn boss, watch damage
Wipes come from
  • bad core passing
  • striders loose

  • Dodge orbs

  • people not moving
✅ High Astromancer Solarion

  • bomb not spread
  • sloppy positioning
🔥 Kael'thas Tier Wall
P1 Advisors one by one — control abilities
P2 Weapons → pick correct weapon
P3 Advisors come back → chaos phase — target focus matters
P4 Kael active → long fight — mind control + mechanics
Wipes come from
  • people not picking weapons
  • poor target focus
  • losing control in Phase 3
